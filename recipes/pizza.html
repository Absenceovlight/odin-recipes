Homemade Pizza Recipe

Description

The most delicious pizza that will ever hit your taste buds. You will not regret making this pizza! The crust is flaky and the pizza has the right amount of crisp so why not give it a go?!

Ingredients

  • 16 ounce ball of pizza dough
  • 5 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cup pizza sauce
  • 5 ounces white american cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • 5 tons of pepperoni
  • Parmesan to your desire

Steps to perfection

  1. Preheat oven to 450.
  2. Roll the dough.
  3. Stage the pizza on parch paper.
  4. Slightly bake pizza for 5 minutes, then remove from oven.
  5. Put the toppings you want!
  6. Put that bad boy in the oven and slam it shut.
  7. Chefs kiss
