Grilled Asparagus

Description

For the vegetarians out there this is for you! This will be the tastiest asparagus you've ever had. Just sit back and enjoy how simple this recipe truly is! It's got just the right amount of char and burn on it to give that smoke taste.

Ingredients

  • Asparus spears 3 pounds
  • A spoonful of olive oil
  • Seasoning of choice

Steps to perfection

  1. Get the grill going.
  2. Coat the spears with olive oil and the seasonings you wanted.
  3. Cook spears for 5 to 10 minutes or until desired look.
