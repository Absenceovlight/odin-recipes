Best Hamburger Ever

Description

The juciest burger you will ever taste. Every bite will have an explosion of flavor from the meat. It's super simple to make and takes not a ton of time compared to many other dishes you could've made

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 2 pounds of bacon
  • Your desired cheese
  • 1 egg
  • garlic
  • 1 spoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper

Steps to perfection

  1. Get the grill fired up.
  2. Combine all of the ingredients listed above!
  3. Cook each side about 6 to 7 minutes or until the middle is no longer pink.
